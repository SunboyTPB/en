Touhou Project; Sanseiroku(The story between Two World)

《Touhou Project; Sanseiroku(The story between Two World)》is the novel based on Touhou Project.

When the world is destoried, three boys and girls came to strange world at the "last time of life". At that place, they met the new friends, met their enemy. They cooperated with friends, to protect this world, to save their world......

Please attention, there is only Chinese(Simplified) version. The English version is writing.
